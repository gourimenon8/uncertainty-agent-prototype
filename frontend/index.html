System Architecture5 layers
1
Data Ingestion
Polymarket CLOB API · BTC perpetual futures funding rate · ETH/BTC ratio from Binance
2
LLM Ensemble
4 prompt templates (free reasoning, adversarial, base rate, uncertainty elicitation) × 2 models × 2 temperatures. Ensemble variance σ² = epistemic uncertainty proxy.
3
Conformal Calibration
Split conformal prediction (Angelopoulos & Bates 2022). Distribution-free coverage guarantees. No Gaussian assumptions. Valid for heavy-tailed crypto markets.
4
Mispricing Detector
Mismatch score = market-implied confidence − model confidence. Market confidence inferred from probability extremity, order book spread, and volume.
5
Kelly Position Sizing
Uncertainty-adjusted Kelly criterion. Bet fraction scales down when CI is wide — distinguishes confident 60% from uncertain 60%.
Research Contribution
Prediction markets price expected value. They don't price uncertainty about that expected value. A market at 60% where every model agrees is very different from 60% where models span 30–85%. This agent identifies that gap.
Novel combination: Conformal prediction intervals applied to LLM ensemble outputs on decentralized prediction markets. Not previously published.
Key finding: Mismatch score identifies overconfident markets. 2/2 prospective hits on MegaETH markets. 7 short-horizon predictions tracked (May 15–31).
Connection: Extends LLM uncertainty quantification research (Dalal et al., Columbia) into prediction market domain.
